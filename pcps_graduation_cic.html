🔢 How the Graduation Rate is Calculated
Simple view of Virginia adjusted cohort math
Σ  Step 1 — Cohort Count
Start with the adjusted 4-year cohort. For this dashboard, that is 298 students in Class of 2026.
⊙  Step 2 — Rate Formula
Graduation Rate = Graduates ÷ Cohort × 100
261 ÷ 298 × 100 = 87.46%
Non-graduates in this cohort snapshot: 37
↗  Step 3 — Index Impact
Graduation rate carries 15% weight in the index.
87.46 × 0.15 = 13.119 points
