Design constraints · April 2026

Four parameters that govern
every build decision.

These are not preferences. They define who owns what decision, where each role's authority begins and ends, and how data reaches schools.

🔵
Tech lead is data steward — not decision-maker
Runs the query, validates output, loads counts into the board, and pushes school sheets after grade-band confirmation. Does not identify students or add mid-year arrivals without Eboni's direction.
🏫
Principals confirm grade by grade via button click
Counts are pushed to each principal's board view — not emailed. Principals confirm grade bands individually. Each confirmed grade band is an independent push. One school's hold does not block another.
📊
Eboni holds all educational authority and is the notification layer
All eligibility decisions belong to the Director of C&I. She notifies principals to review their boards through her normal channels. She approves mid-year adds via a dedicated panel. Her approval is the system trigger — not an email to Monique.
Approval mechanics

How confirmation and approval
work in the dashboard.

Four distinct approval mechanisms — one per role. Each is designed to be the simplest possible action for that role's context.

🏫
Principal — grade-band confirmation panel
Appears at the top of the principal board view during each identification cycle
1
See grade-by-grade count breakdown

Each grade band listed with the identified student count. Example: "Grade K — 18 students · Grade 1 — 22 students · Grade 2 — 24 students"

2
Approve or flag each grade band individually

Two buttons per grade row.

✓ Approve ⚑ Flag for review

Flag opens a one-line note field. Principal or their designee (AP, data lead) can confirm on their behalf.

⚡
Each approved grade band triggers its own push

Grades K–2 approved at Cool Spring push immediately — independent of grade 3–5 or any other school. No waiting.

✓
Panel shows "School confirmed" when all grade bands approved

Green confirmed state visible to principal, Eboni, and Monique simultaneously. Panel collapses after full confirmation.

📊
Director of C&I — district confirmation view
Live status of all schools and grade bands during identification cycles
1
See real-time confirmation status — all schools

District view shows each school, each grade band, and its current status: Pending / Approved / Flagged. Updates live as principals confirm.

2
See flagged grades with principal notes

Any flagged grade band surfaces with the principal's note. Eboni contacts the principal directly to resolve — outside the system, through her normal channels.

3
Mid-year add approval panel — dedicated section

All pending add requests listed: student ID · school · grade · area of need · submitted by · date. Individual or batch approve.

✓ Approve ✓ Approve all
⚡
Approval is the system trigger

When Eboni approves a mid-year add: row added to school tab · reading lead assigned · five fields initialized · reading lead notified · rollup summary updated. Monique not involved for routine adds.

🔵
Tech lead — admin sheet confirmation view
Live status table for push script timing — per school, per grade band
1
See confirmation status per school per grade band

Admin sheet table updates live as principals confirm in the board. Columns: School · Grade Band · Status · Confirmed By · Date. Each row turns green on approval.

2
Run push script per school when all grade bands are green

Each school operates independently. Cool Spring all green → push Cool Spring. Vernon Johns still pending → hold Vernon Johns. No school waits on another.

⚡
Mid-year adds appear in the log — no action needed

When Eboni approves a mid-year add, the row appears in Monique's admin sheet log automatically. She sees the record. If a script error occurs, the error log flags it for her manual trigger.

📚
Reading lead — dashboard notification
How the reading lead knows when a mid-year student has been approved and added
1
Notification badge appears in dashboard caseload view

When Eboni approves a mid-year add, a notification appears in the reading lead's view: "[Student ID] has been added to your caseload at [School]."

2
New student row visible immediately

The student appears in the caseload with five audit fields initialized to Pending. The reading lead's next action: share the form link with the relevant teacher — same as any other student.

⚡
Rollup summary updates in Eboni's panel

Each approval updates a running summary in Eboni's mid-year panel: school name · total mid-year adds · most recent add date. No manual tracking required.

Tech lead · 3-cycle process

Three times a year.
Same process each time.

Outside these three cycles, the tech lead's system involvement is the Monday error check (2 min), directed mid-year adds (5 min each), and mapping table updates when staff changes (2 min). Everything else runs automatically.

Cycle 1
September
Beginning-of-year VALLSS complete
🔵Run query — all K–8 students meeting VLA thresholds
✅Validate — remove duplicates and anomalies
📊Load grade-band counts into principal board views
⏳Eboni notifies principals to review and confirm
📋Monitor admin sheet — push per school as each goes fully green
Cycle 2
January
Mid-year VALLSS screening complete
🔵Run query — net-new students only, not already in system
✅Validate — cross-check against existing school sheets
📊Load net-new counts into principal board views
⏳Eboni notifies principals to confirm net-new additions
📋Push net-new students per school on confirmation
Cycle 3
May
SOL results released
🔵Run query — students newly identified from spring SOL
✅Validate — flag these students for next year's cycle
📊Load counts into principal board views
⏳Eboni determines scope — end-of-year vs. next-year action
📋Push on Eboni's direction — next-year list archived separately

Flag definitions

What surfaces in the dashboard —
what it means, and who resolves it.

Flags appear in dashboard views for the roles that can act on them. The Monday summary is the only automated notification. No push notifications reach school-level roles except the reading lead's mid-year add badge.

📊
Grade band awaiting principal confirmation
Visible to: Director of C&I · Tech Lead (admin sheet)
Counts loaded into principal board but the principal has not yet approved that grade band. The grade band does not push until confirmed.
Eboni follows up with principal · principal clicks Approve in their board
Resolves when: principal clicks Approve for that grade band
⚑
Grade band flagged by principal
Visible to: Director of C&I with principal's note
Principal flagged a grade band as potentially incorrect. Note visible in Eboni's district view. That grade band holds — other grade bands at the same school are unaffected.
Eboni reviews with principal and tech lead · corrected count confirmed · principal re-approves
Resolves when: principal approves the corrected grade band
📋
Plan not yet submitted
Visible to: Director of C&I · Reading Lead
Student is in the dashboard but no form submitted. All five fields show Pending. No letter exists.
Reading lead confirms teacher has the form link and has submitted
Resolves when: teacher submits the form
👩‍🏫
Co-ownership not yet confirmed
Visible to: Director of C&I · Reading Lead
Plan submitted and reading lead auto-assigned but reading lead has not confirmed co-ownership. Field 2 open.
Reading lead opens dashboard and confirms their caseload
Resolves when: reading lead confirms in dashboard
🤝
Parent response pending — 14+ days
Visible to: Director of C&I · Reading Lead
Letter generated more than 14 calendar days ago and no parent response logged. Field 4 open. Included by school in Monday summary.
Reading lead follows up with teacher · logs any response status
Resolves when: reading lead logs any response status
🗂️
Refusal documentation required
Visible to: Director of C&I · Data Coordinator
Parent declined services but Field 5 still open. Written documentation not yet on file. Highest-priority flag — incomplete legal record.
Reading lead uploads signed refusal form · data coordinator confirms
Resolves when: data coordinator confirms documentation on file
🔄
Monitoring update overdue
Visible to: Director of C&I · Reading Lead
No monitoring update logged within 12 days of last update. VLA requires every 10 days. Two-day grace period before flag appears.
Reading lead logs a monitoring update
Resolves when: reading lead logs any monitoring update
👤
Mid-year add pending Eboni's approval
Visible to: Director of C&I — dedicated panel
Reading lead submitted an add request. Request sits in Eboni's approval panel with student ID, school, grade, area of need, submitted by, and date. Does not appear to Monique or reading lead until approved.
Eboni reviews · clicks Approve · system adds row automatically · reading lead notified
Resolves when: Eboni approves — student appears in reading lead's caseload
⚠️
Script error
Visible to: Tech Lead · admin sheet only
A script did not complete. Error logged in plain English — script name, date, description. No school-facing role sees this.
Tech lead opens admin sheet · reads error · clicks manual trigger
Resolves when: script runs successfully on manual trigger
